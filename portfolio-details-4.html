Portfolio Details

  • Project Date: Oct 2024 - January 25
  • Company: Adira Finance
  • Category: Data Analytics Project
  • Project URL: Self-Assessment Tools for Credit Analyst

Portfolio Description

Description: Developed a data-driven monitoring system to track credit policy deviations and risk events, aimed at improving credit approval quality and compliance.

Key Contributions:

  • Collected and processed raw data from internal systems using Excel
  • Performed data cleaning, including handling missing values and removing duplicates
  • Transformed data using Excel functions such as IF, INDEX-MATCH, and array formulas
  • Built monitoring and analysis framework to identify trends in credit performance and policy deviations
  • Developed tables and dashboards to support credit risk monitoring and decision-making

Impact:

  • Reduced risk event rate from 1.52% to 0.44% of total applications
  • Increased Credit Analyst (CA) awareness and adherence to credit policies
  • Improved credit approval accuracy and overall compliance
  • Supported more efficient and data-driven decision-making
